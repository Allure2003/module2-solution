Few of Filipinos' Traits

Makabayan
(Patriotic)
Each breaking day evokes renewed hope. Our farmers draw strength and vigor from the promise of bountiful harvest and abundant takings from our verdant fields and rich waters.

Just us our love for country must continue burning, so is our passion in cultivating our soul - the land of our birth, and from which our food and nourishment come.
Bayanihan
(Communal Unity)
The sense of communal unity and cooperation lives on among Filipinos. This is particularly evident among fishers in our archipelagic country, who would set sail in groups, and help each other haul in nets full of bounty from the seas.

Bayanihan promotes a culture of collaboration and empowerment of every individual throgh teamwork, innovation, and action.
Mapakalinga
(Caring)
The Filipinos' warm hearts and caring hands extend concerns and kindness towards kin, friends, and strangers alike.

With the same tenderness, flower farms are nurtured, The beauty of the blooms now promotes tourism and agriculture in various parts of the country and pays homage to our caring nature.
